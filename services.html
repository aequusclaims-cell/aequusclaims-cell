About Right to Appraisal (RTA)

When an insurance company refuses to pay a fair amount for a legitimate repair, policyholders in most states have the right to invoke the appraisal clause in their policy — a formal dispute process that bypasses the adjuster and brings in independent appraisers to determine the correct value.

The Expert tier is specifically designed with RTA in mind. As a licensed repair dispute appraiser, Oscar prepares every Expert estimate so that if the insurer refuses to pay correctly, the documentation, procedures, and supporting evidence are already assembled and ready to support an RTA filing. You are not starting over — you are already prepared. From the first estimate line to final resolution, Aequus Claims is on your side.
